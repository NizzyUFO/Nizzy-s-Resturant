About SAVORA

Founded in 2019, SAVORA began as a simple idea: to bridge the gap between food lovers and their favorite restaurants. Today, we're proud to be one of the leading food delivery platforms, serving millions of customers across the nation with our commitment to quality, speed, and exceptional service.
